# Navigation using Low Eart Orbit

Also known as LEO-PNT (Low Earth Orbit Position Navigation and Timing)

Triggered by the NewSpace concept (low budget satellites)


## Existing constellations

- [Geely/Geespace](https://zgh.com/geespace/?lang=en), targeted to autonomous driving, lane-level navigation (10 cm horizontal)

Geely Future Mobility Constellation

Additional, NB-IoT support to V2X with dual frequencies L and Ka-band

200 satellites
First 9 in June 2022

- Xona Space Systems, three phase launch 40, 70 and 300

- Centispace (Beijing Future Navigation Technology), 120 sats at 950 km (L1 and L5 satellite system). Satellite CentiSpace-1

- DARPA's Blackjack (US Space Development Agency)
- TBD (United Arab Emirates) 96 satellits at 800 km
